Chairman Dept of CSE RU

Our beloved Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) Dept is soon going to celebrate its 25 years journey on the green campus of Rajshahi University. We are very pleaaed to inform everyone of the CSE family that on this occasion CSE is planning to arrange a grand reunion of all ex and present students of this department. Besides this, CSE also wants to share its awards, achievements and important activities with all. In this regard CSE is going to develop a database of all CSE students starting from the birth of this dept.

In the following webpage CSE has included a form to enter the required information of a student. CSE is hopeful that everyone of our family will cordially come forward and help us in achieving our goal and arrange a successful ceremony.
